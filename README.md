🔥 Steam Consumption Optimization in Manufacturing (ITC Project)

📌 Project Overview

This project is a comprehensive operational deep-dive conducted within ITC Limited’s manufacturing facilities, focusing on steam consumption optimization in the Primary Manufacturing Department (PMD).

It spans the entire value chain — from production processes to resource management — with the objective of improving energy efficiency and operational performance in tobacco manufacturing.

🎯 Objectives

Understand boiler operations and related working principles
Analyze key processes in the primary manufacturing department
Quantify:
Water added & removed
Heat requirements
Steam consumption across units
Establish ideal steam consumption benchmarks
🏭 Process Coverage


The study includes detailed analysis of the following manufacturing units:


DCCC (Drying & Conditioning Chamber)

Lamina Dryer

Admoisture Unit

Stem Dryer

📊 Key Results

Process Unit	Steam Consumption (kg)

DCCC	241.9 kg

Lamina Dryer	691.05 kg

Admoisture	147.82 kg

Stem Dryer	683.46 kg

Total (PMD)	1764.23 kg
